Critical Element Decisions
1. Did the facility provide specialized care needs for respiratory care including tracheostomy care and tracheal suctioning, in accordance with professional standards of practice, and the resident's care plan, goals, and preferences?
⚠ Cite F695
2. Did the facility ensure the therapist(s) providing respiratory services was qualified, had training, and competencies to provide the service?
⚠ Cite F826
N/A: No concerns were identified, or respiratory services were not provided by a respiratory therapist(s).
3. Did the resident's physician delegate the task of writing respiratory therapy orders to a qualified respiratory therapist, and provide supervision?
⚠ Cite F715
N/A: The respiratory therapist did not write respiratory orders.
4. Did staff use appropriate infection control practices, such as hand hygiene and PPE when providing tracheostomy and/or ventilation care, and/or during high-contact care activities?
⚠ Cite F880
5. For newly admitted residents: baseline care plan within 48 hours with written summary the resident could understand?
⚠ Cite F655
N/A: No admission since previous survey OR care/service not necessary in baseline care plan.
6. Comprehensive assessment of physical, mental, and psychosocial needs when condition/risks were present?
⚠ Cite F636
N/A: Condition/risks identified after assessment and didn't meet criteria for significant change MDS, or recently admitted and assessment not yet required.
7. Significant change assessment completed within 14 days of determining status change was significant?
⚠ Cite F637
N/A: Initial comprehensive assessment not yet completed OR no significant change in status.
8. Does the most recent resident assessment accurately reflect the resident's status?
⚠ Cite F641
9. Did the facility develop and implement a comprehensive person-centered care plan with measurable objectives, timeframes, goals, and preferences?
⚠ Cite F656
N/A: Comprehensive assessment was not completed.
10. Did the facility reassess the effectiveness of interventions and review/revise the care plan (with resident input) as necessary?
⚠ Cite F657
N/A: Comprehensive assessment not completed OR care plan not developed OR care plan did not have to be revised.
